Theme Three

Glossy and matte finishes play a game of hide and seek in this stream lined home interiors.

Classic living room with earthy color palette set the Contemporary tone of the home. Pop culture art add a dash of whim to an otherwise quintessential space. Stone TV Panel, textured rug and seating furniture create a cozy space to unwind. As you move to the kitchen, you will come across distinct wood laminates set against a solid white backdrop.The textured wood breaks the monotony and adds texture.

Meanwhile, wardrobes with gloss and matte finish take over the bedrooms. The glossy reflective finish creates an illusion of space and adds depth. The master bedroom has matte finishes that gives a timeless look. The space under the staircase has been efficiently utilized by creating a study area. A study table, book shelf and a lamp fits snugly and unifies the space.
